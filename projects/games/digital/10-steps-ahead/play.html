10 Steps Ahead

An arcade puzzle game with global highscores


(the APK works better; download below)


An Android-port-in-progress of an arcade puzzle game I made in 2020.

Try to survive for as long as possible. If even one attack from the totem hits you through the ring of barriers, you are af!
