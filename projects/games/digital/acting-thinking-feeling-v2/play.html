Acting, Thinking, Feeling

A cooperative card game about resolving emotional conflict



An experimental game that aims to translate the way we experience strong emotions, the reactions we have to them, and the resulting behavioural patterns into a card game.

So far, the game has been a proof-of-concept for translating emotional dynamics to game mechanics, but in quite a mechanical way. While a mechanical base is unavoidable for games with any kind of rules, I'd like the game to lean more on the player's intuition rather than their cognition.

Additionally, I want it to have a bit more of a narrative theme.
